# 十神关系图 · 乙木日主

You are a professional infographic designer. Create an infographic in hand-drawn educational style (hand-drawn-edu).

## Layout: hub-spoke
Center element radiates to surrounding elements with connecting lines. Main concept in center, related sub-concepts around it in a circular/spoke arrangement.

## Style: hand-drawn-edu
Macaron pastel colors, hand-drawn wobble lines, stick figures, chalk-like textures. Warm, approachable, educational. Clean white background with slight texture.

## Aspect Ratio: portrait (9:16) — suitable for mobile reading

## Content Structure

"乙木日主" — warm green background (#69db7c), hand-drawn circle
